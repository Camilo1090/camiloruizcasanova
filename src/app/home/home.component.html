About Me

Hello there! I am a game developer who really likes games too, be it tabletop or digital, cards or sports, I like to play and try all kinds of games.

I initially started my programming career because I really liked computers and I was amazed by computer programs in general. I already liked video games and I thought about becoming a game programmer but that wasn't my main objective by the time, I just wanted to solve problems through code. Later in my last year of undergrad, I had the opportunity to focus my degree project on video games. I, together with some other co-students, developed a series of movement based mini games to assist physical rehabilitation therapies from a local hospital in our city. The objective was to motivate patients to perform their therapies, as they would be playing a game, but also doing some of their exercises. I honestly think it was a really significant and enriching experience, it allowed me to experience working with others as a team, as well as interacting with the patients and physiotherapists from the hospital. I really liked the fact that I could help someone with a problem and also develop a video game.

I decided to go full on becoming a video game developer, making games in any context possible, be it just for entertainment or for a greater cause. I really believe video games have a special power on people, like the ability to inspire emotions.
